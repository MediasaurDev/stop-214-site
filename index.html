About Us

Stop 214 is a brand-new indie games studio founded by Oliver Watkins and Daniel Dewfall, two recent graduates of the University of Portsmouth . Our games will push to create short but sweet and immersive experiences that prioritize polish over profit. Follow our social media for the latest updates, and be sure to come back to see what we cook up.

Why 214?

The name Stop 214 originates from a Japanese fast-food joint where we had our first discussion about starting a studio together after seeing a movie at the cinema. This is considered the inception point for Stop 214. We believe that Stop 214 is a non-tangible place for you to sit down and reflect, and maybe learn something new about yourself.
